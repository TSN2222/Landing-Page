Emojis Are Amazing
Texting cannot always convey tone, emotion, facial expressions, gestures, body language, and any other physical/verbal aspect of communication. Emojis are a great way to help add context to your text message. This way you can more accurately depict what you are trying to say.
My Favorite Emojis!
The "Cool" Emoji. I use this emoji frequently, because I am very cool.
The "Crying" Emoji. I use this emoji frequently, because it's a good reaction.
The "Smiling" Emoji. I use this emoji frequently when something is funny.
The "Sleeping" Emoji. I use this emoji frequently, because I am always tired.
There is been countless times where I have sent a text message and it had been interpreted in a way that I did not intent. Adding emojis helps lighten the mood of a text. Even just the presence of an emoji in a message makes it's content less serious. The use of emojis is typically not professional, so I would only use them in appropriate situations.
